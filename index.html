AOE II: From Beginner to Early Game Expert

Introduction to the project

The following is an in-depth guide which was created for a university DITA project. Three months later, it was then converted to this markdown version.

  • Version: 1.1
  • Author: Andréï Georgescu
  • Initial release: 4 March, 2022
  • Last update: 12 June, 2022

If you have any questions, please feel free to email me at andreigeorgescu.pro@gmail.com

What is Age of Empires II?

Age of Empires II is a 20-year-old real-time strategy game. The game was last re-released in 2019, with updated graphics. Playing a game takes around 20 to 30 minutes.
In regular games, the goal is to create a stronger army than your opponents. To do this, you first have to harvest resources and build a base. Your buildings then allow you to create units, and eventually an army.

Who is this guide for?

This guide targets two profiles:

  • Players who have never played this game or any similar games.
  • Players who have prior experience with the game or similar games and want to get better.

How to navigate this guide

The present guide includes 3 main chapters:

  • Chapter 1 is the introduction.
  • Chapter 2 presents the basics.
    If you are either new to this game or this game genre, you should read chapter 2 before chapter 3.
  • Chapter 3 teaches you how to master early games.
    If you have played this game before and already understand the basics, you can skip right to chapter 3.

To access the two other chapters of this guide, use the dropdrown menu named "AOE II guide" on the top of your screen.
